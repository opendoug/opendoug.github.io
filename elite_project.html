Elite Property Management

Elite came to us looking for some help improving the rental listings on their website. After investigating, we found that they were locked into an iframe by their property management software vendor. This initial investigation, however, launched us down the path of getting Elite access to and ownership of as much of their data as possible! Since our initial engagement, WaltersWorks has helped Elite to improve their web presence and engineer data connectors to give them full control over their company's data.

Solutions:

  • WordPress for improved web presence
  • Google Analytics for traffic measurement
  • Google Tag Manager for conversion measurement
  • Fivetran for data extraction and transformation
  • Google Cloud Platform for custom data engineering
  • Periscope for business intelligence & reporting
  • Sisense for business intelligence & reporting
